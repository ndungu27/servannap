About Servanna

Servanna was founded in 2025 with a mission to revolutionize how people access professional services. We recognized the challenges individuals and businesses face in finding reliable, qualified professionals for their needs.

Our Story

Servanna was created to address a fundamental problem in modern society the difficulty of connecting skilled professionals with people who need their services. Too often, individuals and businesses struggle to find trustworthy experts for essential services, leading to wasted time, frustration, and subpar results.

Our platform was designed to bridge this gap by creating a reliable ecosystem where verified professionals can showcase their expertise and customers can easily access quality services. By establishing rigorous verification standards and a transparent rating system, we ensure that every interaction on our platform meets the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

The societal impact of Servanna extends beyond convenience. We're creating economic opportunities for skilled workers, reducing unemployment in technical fields, and helping small businesses grow by providing them with reliable service providers. Our platform also promotes fair pricing and prevents exploitation by establishing clear service standards and transparent pricing models.

Our Mission

To empower skilled professionals and make quality services accessible to everyone. We believe that by connecting talent with opportunity, we can build stronger communities and drive economic growth.

Connecting Customers & Experts

Similar to how Uber connects riders with drivers, Servanna seamlessly connects customers with certified experts:

  • Real-time Matching: Our intelligent algorithm matches you with the nearest available expert based on your location and service requirements
  • Rating System: Both customers and experts rate each other after service completion to maintain quality standards
  • Live Tracking: Track your expert's arrival in real-time for complete transparency
  • Secure Payments: All transactions are processed securely through our platform

Our Expert Verification Process

We maintain the highest standards by implementing a rigorous 5-step verification process for all experts:

  1. Document verification (ID, certifications, licenses)
  2. Background and reference checks
  3. Skill assessment tests
  4. Mock service simulation
  5. Initial probation period with customer ratings
